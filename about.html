About Us

An idea to give people the news and nothing but!

Volt Report was born out of a desire to cut through the noise and deliver clean, focused updates. What started as a small side project grew into a dedicated space for EV news, with a special spotlight on Tesla. This is meant to be ad free and works great within the in car browser too!

Since purchasing my Tesla Model 3, sustainability has become more than a buzzword — it’s a guiding principle for my life. Driving the Model 3 brought clarity to my goals, inspiring me to create a news platform that promotes innovation, renewable energy, and the future of clean transportation.

Volt Report curates articles from trusted sources using the NewsData API. Whether you're here for Tesla updates, EV industry breakthroughs, or helpful resources like Tesla manuals and guides, this site is designed to keep you informed and inspired.

Thank you for joining me on this journey toward a more sustainable future.
